Meet Our Founder & CEO – Luca Palmeri

Luca Palmeri is a seasoned energy professional and entrepreneur with over 22 years of international experience across gas, LNG, and power markets. As the founder and CEO of MCND Consulting OÜ, Luca brings a unique blend of technical expertise and commercial acumen, having held senior roles in leading energy companies and startups.

Luca is highly skilled in managing cross-cultural teams, navigating complex regulatory environments, and delivering tailored consulting for energy market entry, asset optimization, and contractual structuring.

Professional Highlights

  • Extensive experience in LNG trading, business development, project management, and market analysis.
  • Proven track record in establishing new energy market entities, designing gas and power products, and optimizing asset utilization.
  • Strong network built through direct collaboration with major players including Shell, Exxon, Equinor, Chevron, and key Transmission System Operators across Europe.

Key Projects

Optimization and phase-out of Appygas energy software platform 2022–2025

Our Services

Gas Market Analysis & Trading Strategy

Leverage deep market knowledge and data-driven insights to optimize gas trading and portfolio management. We support risk management, price forecasting, and market entry strategies based on extensive experience with Central, Eastern, and Western European gas hubs.

Key Projects

  • Market analysis and volume growth strategies for Austrian Virtual Trading Point (VTP) and PEGAS products
  • Development of the JKM LNG financial futures contract for the European Energy Exchange

LNG Value Chain Advisory

Guidance through the entire LNG value chain — from contracting and shipping to regasification and portfolio management. We optimize LNG asset usage, forecast demand, and advise on contract negotiations backed by direct experience managing LNG terminal projects and long-term commercial agreements.

Key Projects

  • Project manager and lead negotiator for GATE LNG Terminal in Rotterdam
  • Due diligence and commercial strategy for Cameron LNG Terminal, USA, and Damietta SEGAS LNG Terminal, Egypt
  • Structuring and negotiation of LNG swap agreements and tolling contracts

Power Market & Cross-Commodity Consulting

Expert insights into power market dynamics, renewable integration, and cross-commodity optimization between gas and power sectors. Tailored advisory for project development, regulatory compliance, and market participation.

Key Projects

  • Mid-term procurement and portfolio optimization for combined cycle gas turbine (CCGT) power plants in Romania and Turkey
  • Development and market access strategies for new gas and power products in Central and Eastern Europe

Regulatory & Market Entry Support

Navigate complex regulatory environments and compliance challenges with expert support. We facilitate market entry across Europe, including legal entity establishment, local registrations, and regulatory approvals.

Key Projects

  • Supported Eastern European and Turkish energy companies in establishing Austrian and Estonian legal entities
  • Led incorporation and joint venture setup for Romanian Gas Hub, working closely with Transgaz (Romanian TSO)

Project Development & Asset Optimization

Comprehensive support for energy infrastructure projects, including technical evaluations, stakeholder management, and economic feasibility assessments. Our experience spans LNG terminals, gas pipeline capacities, and power generation assets.

Key Projects

  • Feasibility assessments and infrastructure optimization for multiple LNG terminals including GATE, Cameron, and Damietta SEGAS
  • Asset and contract portfolio management throughout OMV Trading's gas and power operations

Meet Our Founder & CEO – Luca Palmeri

Luca Palmeri is a seasoned energy professional and entrepreneur with over 22 years of international experience across gas, LNG, and power markets. As the founder and CEO of MCND Consulting OÜ, Luca brings a unique blend of technical expertise and commercial acumen, having held senior roles in leading energy companies and startups.

Luca is highly skilled in managing cross-cultural teams, navigating complex regulatory environments, and delivering tailored consulting for energy market entry, asset optimization, and contractual structuring.

Professional Highlights

  • Extensive experience in LNG trading, business development, project management, and market analysis.
  • Proven track record in establishing new energy market entities, designing gas and power products, and optimizing asset utilization.
  • Strong network built through direct collaboration with major players including Shell, Exxon, Equinor, Chevron, and key Transmission System Operators across Europe.

Key Projects

Optimization and phase-out of Appygas energy software platform 2022–2025
Development of G-Compass, Fluxys corporate gas market solution 2022–2024
LNG JKM Future product development for European Energy Exchange 2017–2020
Romanian Gas Hub establishment in partnership with Transgaz 2018–2019
OMV acquisition of EconGas, including integration of LNG, gas & power portfolio 2014–2015
Negotiations and project management of major LNG terminal projects like GATE (Rotterdam), Cameron LNG (USA), Damietta SEGAS LNG Terminal (Egypt). 2014–2015
